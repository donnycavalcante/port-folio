002 : EARTH HEALTHY

The UX Challenge

The ton of garbage yearly produced by Australian university students is a problem that affects all society. During the three year degree, the Australian students producing an excessive quantity of waste, such as printing student materials, works, and copying papers. A successful digital design solution is capable to raise student awareness about recycling.

"Ideation is the process where you generate ideas and solutions through sessions such as Sketching, Prototyping, Brainstorming etc."

Interaction Design Foundation

The UX Approach

The brainstorming and mind mapping techniques were the skills chosen to let the mind produce concepts. After exhaustive work expanding ideas, the convergent design process thinking was applied to narrow down the more applicable concepts related to the target problem: recycling by University students. I have concluded by User Research (Interviews, Personas, Journey Map, Wireframes) that design a website would be a good solution. In addition, the Usability Test (A/B test and Eyetracking) was applied and confirmed the product´s efficiency.

The Final Product

“Earth Healthy” is an educational website. The objective of that webpage is to encourage university students to preserve the environment through recycling. Our final product is an educational website with a User-Friendly interface. I designed and developed the website using tools such as Adobe XD, InVision, and Visual Studio Code.
