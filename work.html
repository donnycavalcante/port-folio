001 : ENHANCING A BOARDING PASS

The UX Challenge

Boarding passes for airline travel have been around for decades, serving billions of passengers within that time, but many would argue their designs are in need of some UX assistance. Given that different airlines and airports take different approaches to their boarding pass design, I unfortunately find ourselves as users confronted with a confusing and inconsistent display of information.

"If I had an hour to solve a problem I'd spend 55 minutes thinking about the problem and 5 minutes thinking about solutions"

Albert Einstein

The UX Approach

The strategy consists of applying Visual Design Principles to create a good User Interface and User Experience. Initially, I started sketching and brainstorming. What design would be possible for our boarding pass? After many sketches, I figured out some design to our digital boarding pass.

The Final Product

A Digital Boarding pass and an iPad application were the final results. After I applied the Gestalt Principles and their visual perception theories, I made efforts to enhance the design. I applied the law of figure to ground and the principle of 'common region' to digitally group elements with the same pattern on the screen. In this way, the User Experience will be more intuitive, easier, and pleasant.
